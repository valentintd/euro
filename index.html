Did you know ? History of perspective

Did you know? That the Egyptians used perspective in another way by using size to indicate importance, this is called the hieratic scale.

Did you know? In the Byzantine Empire Between 476 and 1453, the reverse perspective was create in which the objects depicted in a scene are placed between the projective point and the viewing plane. Objects farther away from the viewing plane are drawn as larger, and closer objects are drawn as smaller, in contrast to the linear perspective for which closer objects appear large.

Did you know? That in Pompeii, the second style was born. But what is the second style? Well, it is a technique that shifts the perspective in the room, the visitor's eye moves continuously in the room.

Did you know? In 1862, a new perspective was create where colors and form lose their clarity the further they are from us

Did you know that? Cubism broke the rules of linear perspective. Well yes, it consists in representing an object from multiple perspectives.
